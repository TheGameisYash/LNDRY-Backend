# LNDRY Asset Pack

This is the consolidated, approved LNDRY V2 delivery pack.

## Open First

- `07-Preview-Boards/production-pack.png` — visual overview of banners and customer screens.
- `07-Preview-Boards/service-icon-contact-sheet.png` — required service icon family.
- `08-Design-Docs/DESIGN.md` — visual rules and tokens.
- `08-Design-Docs/ASSET_MANIFEST.md` — inventory and scope rules.
- `08-Design-Docs/SCREEN_COVERAGE_MATRIX.md` — exact screen-by-screen source and raster status.
- `08-Design-Docs/ASSET_VALIDATION_REPORT.md` — final integrity, dimension, and completion evidence.

## Folder Guide

1. `01-Logos` — careline symbol, horizontal wordmark, and app icon in editable SVG.
2. `02-Service-Icons` — the 11 required transparent SVG service icons.
3. `03-3D-Illustrations` — transparent service and journey PNG assets.
4. `04-Banners` — three production in-app banners.
5. `05-UI-Mockups` — customer journey, authentication, support, and state screens.
6. `06-State-Illustrations` — editable SVG masters for empty, offline, payment-failure, and service-area states.
7. `07-Preview-Boards` — quick visual contact sheets.
8. `08-Design-Docs` — product, design-system, inventory, gap-audit, and screen-coverage documentation.
9. `09-Deployment-Assets` — adaptive icon layers, notification icon, iOS/web masters, map marker, and image fallbacks.
10. `10-UI-Source-Specs` — deterministic customer, vendor, delivery employee, and admin source screens used for accurate exports.
11. `11-WebP-and-Flutter` — optimized WebP cutouts, banner exports, and Flutter 1x/2x/3x asset structure.
12. `12-Vendor-Mockups` — 19 application, setup, order, processing, audit, and performance screens.
13. `13-Rider-Mockups` — 14 pickup, Maps handoff, OTP, return, delivery, and recovery screens.
14. `14-Admin-Mockups` — 17 secure access, verification, operations, configuration, report, and audit screens.
15. `15-Store-Media` — Google Play feature graphic, six Play screenshots, and six Apple App Store 6.9-inch screenshots.

## Approved Scope

- Existing LNDRY UI sheets remain the source of truth.
- No speculative garment, navigation, trust, or status icon families are included.
- New icons should be added only when implementation proves a real screen needs one.
- All approved assets use violet as the brand identity and teal only for verified, available, successful, or in-progress states.

## Rejected Content

The unrelated muscle-contraction and gospel/zodiac-like chart generations were deleted and are not present in this pack.
