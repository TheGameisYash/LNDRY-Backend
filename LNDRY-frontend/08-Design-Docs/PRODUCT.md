# Product

## Register

product

## Users

LNDRY serves Indian customers who need dependable garment care without spending time calling or comparing individual shops. Core users include busy professionals, working couples, families, students, older adults, first-time online laundry customers, and people seeking urgent or specialist garment care. They are typically choosing a vendor, price, pickup window, and service while balancing trust, time, and uncertainty about how their garments will be handled.

## Product Purpose

LNDRY is a multi-vendor laundry-service marketplace. Customers discover eligible nearby vendors, compare supported services and published rates, choose a vendor, select garments or estimated weight, schedule a 60-minute pickup slot, pay online, and follow an auditable status journey through OTP-verified delivery. Success means the customer can book confidently in under a minute without losing visibility or control.

## Brand Personality

Quietly premium, human, and dependable. LNDRY should make garment care feel considered rather than industrial, local service discovery feel trustworthy rather than chaotic, and operational progress feel calm rather than technical. The emotional arc is relief, confidence, reassurance, and satisfaction.

## Anti-references

- A generic laundry template built from washing-machine clip art, random bubbles, water drops, or stock blue gradients.
- An AI-generated collection of disconnected screens, glossy neon objects, illegible pseudo-text, or inconsistent icon styles.
- A copied Dribbble concept that optimizes decoration over booking clarity.
- A website compressed into a mobile app.
- Baked-in checkerboards pretending to be transparent backgrounds.
- Turquoise glow effects, dark halos, and synthetic 3D assets that conflict with the violet LNDRY identity.
- Unsupported subscription, loyalty, live-rider-map, surge-pricing, or marketing claims.

## Design Principles

1. **Relief before promotion.** Every primary surface should reduce effort or uncertainty before showing an offer.
2. **Marketplace clarity is the product.** Vendor, price basis, distance, availability, completion time, and trust signals must be easy to compare.
3. **Care is visible.** Use the LNDRY care-line motif and clear service/status imagery to show professional handling at every step.
4. **Operational truth builds trust.** Status, estimates, pricing, and eligibility must reflect the approved workflow; never imply live tracking or guarantees the system cannot provide.
5. **One continuous story.** Discovery, booking, pickup, processing, delivery, and reordering must feel like chapters of the same experience.
6. **Production realism.** Assets and components must be reusable, Flutter-friendly, performant, and legible on lower-end Android devices as well as iOS.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.2 AA. Maintain at least 44 x 44 px touch targets, readable contrast, dynamic text resilience, screen-reader labels, and status communication through text and iconography rather than color alone. Support one-handed use, sunlight readability, low-bandwidth fallbacks, reduced motion, long Indian names and addresses, and users with limited digital familiarity.
